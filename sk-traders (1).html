Our Story

About
S.K. Traders

A trusted name in Jaipur's two-wheeler spare parts industry, operating under the flagship brand NAMO Gold Genuine Parts.

Who We Are

The Company

S.K. Traders is a leading wholesale distributor and trader of two-wheeler body parts and spare parts, headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan. We operate from our facility in the RIICO Industrial Area, Sarna Dungar, Benad Road — one of Jaipur's key automotive hubs.

Under our flagship brand NAMO Gold Genuine Parts, we supply body parts, mechanical components, lighting, and accessories for motorcycles and scooters across India.

Every product is sourced under strict quality checks to ensure genuine fitment, long service life, and peak performance — trusted by workshops, mechanics, and retailers throughout Rajasthan.
