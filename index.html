Our Story

About
S.K. Traders

Trusted wholesale distributors of two-wheeler spare parts under the NAMO Gold Genuine Parts brand, Jaipur.

Who We Are

The Company

S.K. Traders is a leading wholesale distributor of two-wheeler body parts, headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan. We operate from the RIICO Industrial Area, Sarna Dungar, Benad Road — one of Jaipur's key automotive hubs.

Under our flagship brand NAMO Gold Genuine Parts, we supply body parts, mechanical components, lighting, and accessories for motorcycles and scooters across India.

Every product is sourced under strict quality checks to ensure genuine fitment, long service life, and peak performance — trusted by workshops, mechanics, and retailers throughout Rajasthan.
